# Skills and Statistics

![Skills](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/arn-c0de/ZombieEscape-Preview/main/images/skills.png)

## Skills

The Skills page shows overall level, total XP, and progress in the main disciplines:

- **Combat** — fighting and weapon use
- **Survival** — staying alive and managing survival needs
- **Scavenging** — exploration and looting
- **Crafting** — creating equipment and supplies
- **Medical** — injury treatment and medical actions
- **Engineering** — technical, construction, and vehicle-related activity

Tap a skill for its details. Skills can be displayed grouped or ungrouped. Performing relevant actions awards XP and improves progression.

## Statistics

![Statistics](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/arn-c0de/ZombieEscape-Preview/main/images/stats.png)

The Stats page includes:

- Overall level and XP
- Skill level and skill XP
- Fist and weapon damage
- Combat skill bonuses and active buffs
- Total armor and approximate damage reduction
- Current survival time
- Zombies killed, games played, and games won
- Distance traveled on foot and in vehicles
- Events collected
- Medical/effect usage and restored health
- Active effects and recent effect log
- Achievements
- Recent inventory activity

Use this page to compare equipment and buffs, track progress, and identify effects that are currently helping or hurting the character.
