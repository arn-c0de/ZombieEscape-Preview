## Opening the workshop

Open **Crafting** from the main menu, inventory, or Quick Actions. Choose **Craft** for equipment and supplies or **Cook** for meals. The recipe list shows how many recipes are ready, the result quantity, crafting time, missing materials, inventory weight, and required station.

## Crafting an item

1. Select a recipe.
2. Review its result, time, materials, and station requirement.
3. Ensure all required materials are in your inventory.
4. Leave enough carry capacity for the result.
5. Press **Craft**.

Materials are taken only when crafting succeeds. The system handles stacked materials and avoids leaving a partial transaction if creation fails.

## Cooking and fire

Cooking recipes may require an active **Campfire** or **Stove** and enough remaining fuel time. Use **Prepare Fire** when a recipe requires a station. Campfires and stoves persist in the world, so they can become useful supply points for repeated expeditions.

Cooking turns ingredients into meals and can improve food value or add effects. The details page lists ingredients, station requirement, fuel availability, time, result quantity, and bonuses.

## Good crafting habits

- Keep common components such as cloth, wood, metal, nails, tape, and chemicals.
- Craft bandages before a dangerous trip.
- Do not consume the last food or water ingredient if you need it immediately.
- Store bulky construction materials at home until needed.
