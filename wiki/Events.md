# Events

## Finding events

The HUD shows the number of active nearby events. Open **Nearby Events** from the interaction menu to see event type, distance, remaining time, radius, and description. Events expire, so compare the reward with the travel time and danger before committing.

## Event types

- **Military Airdrop** — weapons, ammunition, armor, and occasionally medicine
- **Medical Airdrop** — medical supplies, with occasional food or water
- **Civilian Airdrop** — food, water, tools, and occasionally simple weapons
- **Helicopter Crash** — military and police equipment
- **Supply Cache** — mixed survival items
- **Rescue Point** — medical and basic civilian supplies
- **Evacuation** — mixed high-value items
- **Survivor Rescue** — a friendly survivor who may be recruited

## Collecting rewards

Move into the event radius and interact with its marker or nearby action. Leave enough inventory capacity before collecting. High-value events can draw the player away from safety, so carry food, water, medical supplies, ammunition, and fuel appropriate to the trip.

For survivor recruitment, claim a house with a free resident slot before completing the rescue if possible.
